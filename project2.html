📊 Orange Gadget Mobile Sales Dashboard

Power BI Dashboard for Strategic and Tactical Business Insights

🔧 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes mobile phone sales data across brands, cities, and time. It delivers metrics like total sales, quantity, customer satisfaction, and payment behavior, helping decision-makers track trends and act with confidence.

🔶 Page 1: Executive Sales Overview

  • Total Sales: 1.077 Billion
  • Quantity Sold: 19K units
  • Transactions: 3.8K
  • Average Price: 56.2K

These metrics summarize overall business performance efficiently.

📈 Quantity Sold by Month

Highest: March (1696), July (1700) | Lowest: February (1451)
Useful for analyzing seasonality and campaign effectiveness.

🌍 Quantity by City (Map Visualization)

Dhaka, Rajshahi, Chattogram, and Khulna show higher density—ideal for regional planning.

💳 Transactions by Payment Method

Even spread across Bkash, Debit, Credit Cards, and Cash (24–26% each)—indicating the importance of digital payment support.

📅 Quantity Sold by Day

Highest: Friday (679), Lowest: Thursday (553)
Shows weekend shopping behavior.

⭐ Customer Ratings

100% Good rating shown. However, 852 Average/Bad ratings need action for quality improvement.

🔝 Top 3 Models by Quantity Sold

  • iPhone SE – 1430 units
  • OnePlus Nord – 1409 units
  • Galaxy Note 20 – 1382 units

📋 Brand Performance Table

Samsung leads in sales value (224M); Xiaomi excels in quantity. Insightful for pricing and segmenting.

🔶 Page 2: MTD Analysis (Jan 2023)

  • Sales: 33M
  • Quantity Sold: 545
  • Transactions: 110
  • Avg Price: 59.9K

Tracks January in near-real-time for agile strategy shifts.

📈 Sales Growth Trend (Jan)

Steady growth, peak mid-month—possibly influenced by salary dates or promotions.

🔶 Page 3: YoY and Quarterly Analysis

  • 2023 Sales: 355M | LY: 367M
  • Best Quarter: Q3 (93M)
  • Best Months: May, July, August (~34M)
  • Weakest: Feb, Sept (~26M)

Helps in forecasting, budgeting, staffing.

📋 Summary Table

Yearly and quarterly data comparison for smart decision-making.

🧠 Dashboard Benefits

  • 360° sales view
  • Strategic and tactical support
  • Identifies strengths and improvement areas

🛠 Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • DAX, Power Query
  • Map Visuals
  • Data Storytelling

💬 Final Thoughts

This dashboard showcases the ability to transform raw sales data into actionable insights with clean, compelling visuals for real-world business decisions.
