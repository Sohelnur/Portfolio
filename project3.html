This project presents a comprehensive Pizza Sales Dashboard built to provide insightful and interactive visualizations for tracking sales metrics across multiple branches. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders, including business managers and franchise owners, make data-driven decisions and monitor performance at a glance.
